# Week 4: Fridge to Recipe (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** A single text box answering "What's in your fridge?"

**Output:** One recipe (title, ingredients, steps) and an AI-generated photo of the dish.

## 2. The Magical Moment

Practical personalization ("It actually used my random ingredients!").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

Just the recipe. No grocery store mapping, shopping lists, dietary filters, or meal planners.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

The culinary voice of the AI—is it a strict budget planner, a supportive grandma, or a high-end chef?
